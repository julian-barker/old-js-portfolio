About Me

I grew up in southwest Connecticut with my parents and brother. I was always interested in technology and science, but never broke into the self-taught coder life in my youth. I graduated the US Army Ranger School in 2017 and spent the following 5 years in various leadership roles that taught me a lot about leading teams of people, managing valuable resources, and budgeting the most precious commodity of all: time. I recently exited the military (2022) and am now following my dream of learning to code by attending the Code Fellows Software Development bootcamp.
